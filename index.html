F.A.Q

  • Does this event cost money?

    Not at all! It is completely free, thanks to the generous donations by our sponsors

  • What is the theme?

    Build an app or website with a novel approach of using AI & machine learning for social good

  • What is a Hackathon?

    A Hackathon is a 24 hour programming event where students work together to develop a software or hardware project

  • Who can participate?

    Any high school student can participate

  • What if I don't know how to code?

    No problem! We will be hosting several workshops throughout the event intended to help you learn to code

  • What should I bring?

    A laptop and a charger is all that you need. A sleeping bag is recommended (if you plan to sleep 😉)

  • How do I form a team?

    If you do not have a team already, there will be team formation at the beginning of the hackathon. This is an opportunity for you to meet new people and form teams. Each team should have a maximum of four people
